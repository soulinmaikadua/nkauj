Ib ntsais muag xwb wb lub neej tawg tag

Xav tsi tsog tiag wb yuav zoo li no

Koj puag nco tsog wb lub neej qub qab

Kwv tij neej tsa luag nim vam

Zaum no wb cov me nyuam yuav ua cag

Ntse lam muab luag hu tiag niam xwb twb tsi yog koj

Es cov nrog koj mug log ntshe yuav tu lub siab nro

Ntse lam muab luag hu tiag txiv xwb twb tsi yog kuv

Ntse wb tej txheeb ze yuav tsi sib hu ntxiv lawm

Vim wb sib nrauj lawm

Ib ntsais muag xwb luag twb cav nroo ntws

Tias tug yuav koj twb tsi yog kuv lawm

Tu siab kawg los ua tsi tau li cag

Yuav rov qab log li

Zaum no wb cov me nyuam yuav ua ntsuag

Koj niam cag es wb lub neej yuav tsi tsaug ntseeg

Ib npliag ntsis tug tuav koj teg twb tsi yog kuv

Koj niam eg wb lub me neej puag zoo li zaj movie

Es thaum kawg tov qab los sib yuav dua

Ntshe yuav tag mus ib sim

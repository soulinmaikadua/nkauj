ua cas nag hmo rov ua npau suav

rov qab tau puag koj li thaum hluas

wb twb laus tag nyias muaj nyias cuab

los tseem xav kom tiag txhob yog npau suav

<br />

nag hmo es nws yog hmo dab ts

ntshe kuv yuav tau muab ntawv thiab npiv

coj los cuv cia hnub nyoog vas thib

seb txog hmo ntawv puas yuav rov ntsib

<br />

ntshe yuav zoo li tij tuam yaj txoj nkauj

yog ntuj tsis tig ntshe tsis ntsib lawm lau

lam yog daim nam kuv yuav muab kauv

kom rov qab dhia los pib hauv paus

<br />

tiam sis caij nyoog tig tsis tau rov

ntawm koj thiab kuv ces ntshe tsuas yog

zoo caij zoo nyoog seb puas sib pom

hauv nruab dab ntub li hmo ntawv lawm xwb os mog
